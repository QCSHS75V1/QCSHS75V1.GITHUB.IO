Another Web Page for
Quezon City Science High School

This is another web page about the former students of Quezon City Science High School. Everyone is invited to contribute to this web page. I am counting especially on the class of 1975 to help build this web site.

An e-mail group ID containing the addresses of several classmates has been set-up
asmailto:qcshs-75-alumni@yahoogroups.com. E-mail sent (by group members) to this ID will be sent to all group members.
Click on the following to see names of current members: who-is-where

Please join this e-mail group by introducing yourself to the batch (send email to qcshs75@yahoo.com describing who you are). It may take the egroup moderator 1 day or so to register new members. An archive of old E-mail may be viewed (by group members only) at http://www.yahoogroups.com/
